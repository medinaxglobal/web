What Is a Hair Transplant?

A hair transplant is a surgical procedure that moves healthy hair follicles from a donor area — typically the back and sides of the scalp — to areas experiencing thinning or baldness. Modern techniques achieve results that are completely natural-looking, permanent and indistinguishable from untouched hair.

Istanbul has become the global capital of hair restoration, performing more transplants per year than any other city in the world. This extraordinary volume means our surgeons develop a level of expertise that far surpasses what is achievable in lower-volume UK clinics.

Which Technique Is Right for You?

FUE

Follicular Unit Extraction — the gold standard technique. Individual follicles extracted with a micro-punch tool and implanted into the recipient area.

Best for: General hair loss

DHI ★ Recommended

Direct Hair Implantation using the Choi pen tool — simultaneous extraction and implantation for superior density and faster recovery.

Best for: Crown & frontal density

Sapphire FUE

FUE performed with sapphire-tipped blades — creates finer channels, promotes faster healing and is ideal for patients with sensitive scalps.

Best for: Sensitive scalps

What Results Can I Expect?

  • Months 1–3: Initial shedding phase (expected and normal — follicles are settling)
  • Months 3–6: Early regrowth becomes visible, texture begins to form
  • Months 6–10: Significant density improvement, natural hairline shaping
  • Months 10–14: Full results visible — complete, natural, permanent density

The transplanted follicles are genetically resistant to the hormone DHT (the cause of male-pattern baldness), meaning results are genuinely permanent in the vast majority of cases.

Am I a Good Candidate?

Most adults experiencing hair loss are suitable candidates. The key factors our surgeons assess are:

  • Sufficient donor area density at the back and sides of the scalp
  • Stable hair loss pattern (typically Norwood Scale II–VI)
  • Realistic expectations about achievable graft density
  • Good general health with no contraindicated medical conditions
  • Not currently taking blood-thinning medications (which can be paused)

Our free hair assessment will confirm your candidacy and recommend the ideal technique and graft count for your specific situation.

What Is a Mummy Makeover?

A mummy makeover is not a single operation — it is a personalised combination of surgical procedures designed to address the physical changes that pregnancy, childbirth and breastfeeding bring to a woman's body. Most commonly, it targets the breasts, abdomen and waistline, though every treatment plan is tailored uniquely to the individual patient's goals.

It is worth noting that a mummy makeover is not exclusively for mothers. Anyone who has experienced significant weight loss or changes to their body shape may be an excellent candidate for this group of procedures — the underlying surgical principles apply equally.

Importantly, a mummy makeover is a body reshaping procedure, not a weight loss solution. Candidates are generally advised to be at or close to their ideal, stable weight before proceeding. This ensures the most aesthetically balanced, long-lasting result.

⏱
Recovery Time
Most patients return to desk-based work within 2–3 weeks. Full physical activity is typically resumed at 6–8 weeks post-surgery.
🌟
Final Results
Full results become visible at 3–6 months once all post-operative swelling has resolved. Early improvements are noticeable from week 4–6 onwards.
💉
Anaesthesia
A mummy makeover is performed under general anaesthesia administered by a consultant anaesthetist. You will be completely asleep throughout the procedure.
✨
Longevity of Results
Results are long-lasting when maintained with a stable weight. Natural ageing and significant weight fluctuations can affect the outcome over time.

What's Included in a Mummy Makeover?

Your mummy makeover package is built around your specific concerns. While the most common combination addresses the breasts and abdomen, your final treatment plan is decided during your pre-operative consultation with your assigned surgeon.

👙

Breast Augmentation

If you have experienced significant volume loss following breastfeeding, implants can restore fullness and projection. A range of sizes, profiles and implant types are available.

⬆️

Breast Lift (Mastopexy)

If sagging or drooping is your primary concern without significant volume loss, a mastopexy alone repositions the breast tissue and nipple for a more youthful appearance.

✨

Augmentation + Lift

The most commonly requested breast component — combining implants with a lift for both restored volume and elevated, firmer positioning. Ideal for post-breastfeeding changes.

⬇️

Breast Reduction

If pregnancy has increased breast volume causing discomfort, a reduction removes excess tissue to achieve a smaller, better-supported and more proportionate result.

Your surgeon's recommendation: The best breast component for your mummy makeover will be determined during your consultation, based on your current breast shape, skin quality, nipple position and personal goals.

How Is a Mummy Makeover Performed?

Because a mummy makeover combines multiple procedures, careful surgical planning is essential to ensure both safety and optimal aesthetic outcomes. Your Elara Medical surgeon will determine the safest operative sequence based on your specific combination of procedures.

1
Pre-operative AssessmentBlood tests, health screening and a detailed consultation with your surgeon. Photographs are taken and your personalised surgical plan is confirmed and agreed.
2
Anaesthesia & PreparationGeneral anaesthesia is administered. Surgical markings are made and the operating field is prepared to the highest sterile standards.
3
Breast Procedure (if included)The breast component is performed first — whether augmentation, lift, combined augmentation + lift, or reduction — before moving to the abdominal area.
4
Tummy Tuck & Muscle Repair (if included)Excess skin is removed, abdominal muscles are repaired if separated, and the belly button is repositioned. The incision is carefully closed to minimise scarring.
5
Liposuction (if included)Stubborn fat deposits are permanently removed from agreed areas using precision tumescent liposuction technique for optimal contouring results.
6
Recovery & Hospital CareYou will be monitored in hospital for 1–2 nights. Drains, compression garments and medications are provided. Your coordinator checks in daily throughout your Istanbul stay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have a mummy makeover if I plan to have more children?
Technically yes, but it is strongly advised to complete your family before undergoing a mummy makeover. A subsequent pregnancy will stretch the abdominal skin and potentially separate the repaired muscles — reversing the surgical results. We always discuss family planning intentions during your consultation.
How long do I need to stay in Istanbul?
We recommend planning for 7–8 nights in Istanbul for a full mummy makeover. This allows for 1–2 nights in hospital, 5–6 nights of hotel recovery, and clearance from your surgeon before flying home. Some patients with more extensive procedures may be advised to extend slightly.
Will I have visible scars?
All surgery results in scars, and a mummy makeover is no exception. However, your surgeon places incisions strategically so they fall within the natural bikini line, making them easily concealed under underwear and swimwear. Scars fade significantly over 12–18 months and scar management advice is provided throughout your aftercare.
Can I combine a mummy makeover with other procedures?
It is possible to add certain procedures to your makeover package — such as a Brazilian butt lift, arm lift or thigh lift — however, surgical safety is the priority. Our surgeons will advise on the maximum combination appropriate for a single anaesthetic session, and may recommend staging additional procedures as a second surgery if required.
How soon can I exercise after a mummy makeover?
Light walking is encouraged from day 3. Most patients return to gentle, low-impact activity around weeks 4–6. Core exercises, weight training and high-impact activities should not be resumed until approximately 8–10 weeks post-surgery, or when specifically cleared by your surgeon.
Is it safe to travel to Istanbul for this procedure?
All Elara Medical partner hospitals are JCI-accredited — meeting the same international safety standards as the UK's leading private hospitals. Your Questions, Answered Honestly.

We believe in complete transparency. Here is every question we hear from patients — answered without spin, pressure or medical jargon.

Ask Us Directly
General
What exactly is Elara Medical?
Elara Medical is a UK-registered medical tourism facilitator. We are not a clinic or hospital — we act as a trusted intermediary between British patients and Istanbul's finest accredited medical facilities. We vet, book and coordinate your entire treatment journey, managing logistics, translations, aftercare and support throughout.
Is Elara Medical a UK-registered company?
Yes. Elara Medical Ltd is registered in England and Wales and operates fully within UK consumer protection law. Our London office is staffed Monday–Saturday and our team is accountable to British regulatory standards. You are protected by UK law when booking through us.
Can I bring a companion with me?
Absolutely — we actively encourage it. We regularly accommodate patients travelling with a partner, friend or family member. We can arrange hotel accommodation for your companion at the same property, and our VIP transfers are always booked to accommodate at least one accompanying person.
How do I get started?
Simply book a free consultation — by phone, video call or WhatsApp. One of our specialist clinical advisors will discuss your goals, answer every question and send you a personalised treatment plan within 24 hours. There is no obligation to proceed and no pressure whatsoever.
Safety & Medical
How safe is surgery in Istanbul?
Istanbul's JCI-accredited hospitals meet identical international safety standards to the UK's leading private hospitals. Our partner clinics undergo rigorous annual audits, and all surgeons hold international board certification. We personally audit every facility before adding it to our network — your safety is our absolute first priority.
Who will perform my surgery?
You will be assigned a named, board-certified specialist surgeon — chosen specifically for your procedure type. We provide their full credentials, patient rating and surgery portfolio before you confirm your booking. You will have a pre-operative consultation with your surgeon before the procedure begins.
What anaesthesia will I receive?
This depends entirely on your procedure. Hair transplants are performed under local anaesthesia with sedation. Major cosmetic surgery (rhinoplasty, breast, body) uses general anaesthesia administered by a qualified consultant anaesthetist. All patients undergo a pre-operative anaesthetic assessment.
What if I experience complications?
All Elara Medical patients are covered by our comprehensive aftercare guarantee. In the rare event of complications, we coordinate directly with your treating surgeon, arrange urgent follow-up appointments, and in exceptional circumstances, cover revision procedures at no additional cost to the patient.
Costs & Packages
What does "all-inclusive" actually include?
Your all-inclusive package covers: the complete procedure, all pre-operative medical tests, 4 or 5-star hotel accommodation for the specified number of nights, all VIP airport and clinic transfers, a personal patient coordinator, translation services, all post-operative medications, and 12-month aftercare support from our UK team. The only exclusions are your flights and personal spending during your stay.
Are there any hidden costs?
Never. We have a strict no-hidden-costs policy. Before you confirm your booking, you will receive a detailed written quote covering every element of your package. The only variable is your flight cost, which you book independently (we provide guidance on the best routes and timings).
When do I pay, and is a deposit required?
A small deposit (typically £200–£400) is required to confirm your booking and secure your surgeon assignment. The remaining balance is due 14 days before your departure date. We accept all major credit and debit cards, and bank transfer.
Do you offer finance or payment plans?
Yes. We have partnered with a UK-regulated finance provider to offer 0% interest payment plans for eligible patients. Applications are assessed individually and subject to credit status. Please ask your advisor for details during your consultation.
Travel & Logistics
Do I need to book my own flights?
Flights are not included in our standard packages, but we provide you with a full recommended flight itinerary — including the best routes from your nearest airport, recommended departure and return times based on your procedure, and a list of which UK carriers operate direct services to Istanbul.
What hotel will I stay in?
We work with a carefully selected group of 4 and 5-star hotels within easy distance of your clinic. All have been personally inspected by our team and selected for their experience hosting post-operative patients — including appropriate facilities, dietary catering and English-speaking concierge staff.
How many nights will I need to stay?
This depends on your procedure. Hair transplants typically require 3–4 nights. Cosmetic surgery (rhinoplasty, breast, body) requires 5–7 nights. Dental treatments are usually completed in 5–7 days. Bariatric surgery requires 5–7 nights. Your quote will specify the exact recommended duration.
Aftercare & Recovery
What UK aftercare support is provided?
Your dedicated UK patient coordinator will check in with you at 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months and 12 months post-procedure. They are also available by phone, email or WhatsApp at any time in between. For complex procedures, we can arrange local GP review letters and coordinate with your NHS doctor if needed.
When can I return to work?
Most desk-based or remote workers return to work within 5–10 days of their procedure. Those in physically demanding roles should allow 2–4 weeks. Your surgeon will provide you with a personalised return-to-work and activity guidance letter before discharge.
When will I see my full results?
Timelines vary by procedure. Hair transplants: full results at 12–14 months. Rhinoplasty: swelling fully resolved at 6–12 months. Breast surgery: stable result at 6–8 weeks. Body surgery: final result at 3–6 months. Dental work: immediate. Weight loss surgery: ongoing over 12–24 months. Eye surgery: full vision stability within 2–4 weeks.
Hair Transplant
Does a hair transplant hurt?
The scalp is comprehensively numbed with local anaesthetic before the procedure begins. Most patients report mild discomfort during the initial injections, and little to no pain during the transplant itself. Post-operatively, mild tenderness and tightness are common for 3–5 days and are well-managed with the medications provided.
Will my transplanted hair fall out?
Yes — this is expected and completely normal. In the first 2–4 weeks after your transplant, most of the newly implanted hairs will shed. This is called the "shock loss" phase. The follicles remain dormant for approximately 3 months before regrowing permanently. This process is a completely normal part of the transplant cycle.
How many grafts will I need?
This varies based on your Norwood hair loss stage, donor area density and the coverage area. As a general guide: Norwood II–III requires 1,500–2,500 grafts; Norwood IV requires 2,500–3,500 grafts; Norwood V–VI requires 3,500–5,000 grafts. Our free assessment will provide a precise graft count for your individual case.
Can women have hair transplants?
Yes — female hair transplantation is a growing and highly effective treatment for women experiencing pattern hair loss, traction alopecia or hairline recession. The techniques used are identical to male transplants, though the approach, design and graft count are tailored to female hair loss patterns and aesthetic goals.
Dental Treatments
How long do veneers last?
E-Max porcelain veneers typically last 15–20 years with proper care. Zirconia veneers can last 20–25 years or more. Composite veneers last 5–7 years. Our partner clinic uses only E-Max and zirconia materials — we do not offer lower-grade composite options.
Will I have temporary teeth during my visit?
Yes — after preparation on Day 2, temporary restorations are fitted immediately so you never leave the clinic without teeth. These temporaries are well-made and comfortable whilst your permanent restorations are crafted in the laboratory over the following 2–3 days.
Can I eat normally after dental treatment?
You will be advised to avoid very hard foods for the first two weeks following your final fittings while the adhesive cement reaches full strength. After this, your veneers and crowns are fully functional — you can eat normally. Your dentist will provide a full aftercare guide including which foods to enjoy and which to moderate.
